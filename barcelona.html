Casa Milà

The roof of Casa Milà was one of my favorite places in Barcelona. Just sitting on the stairs and enjoying the sunshine, we could spend hours there.

Tibidabo

We were just in time for the sunset when we got to the top of Tibidabo. We could see the whole city of barcelona, the sea, and the horizon far away. I especially love the colorful ferris wheel
